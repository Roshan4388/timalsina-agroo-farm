📜 Our History

Timalsina Agro Farm traces its roots back to the early 1980s when our founder, Nitta Parsad Timalsina, acquired a small plot of land with a vision to cultivate organic crops using traditional methods. Over the next few decades, the farm grew through hard work, family dedication, and a commitment to sustainable practices.

By the 1990s, the farm had already expanded to include irrigation systems and diversified into vegetables, grains, and livestock. The turn of the century brought new technologies, but the Timalsina family remained grounded in their heritage, blending modern irrigation techniques with age-old wisdom.

Today, the farm is a thriving agricultural enterprise, supporting dozens of families in the surrounding community and serving as a model for eco-friendly farming in the region. From humble beginnings to a modern-day success, our history is a testament to perseverance, community, and the timeless value of the land.
